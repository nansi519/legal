Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Welcome to MOOD (“the App”). We value your privacy. This App is designed to provide a serene experience with minimal data footprint. We do not require account registration, and your personal identity remains anonymous to us.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, to provide premium features through In-App Purchases, we collect certain non-identifiable data:

  • Purchase History: We record your subscription status, transaction IDs, and purchase dates provided by Apple.
  • Device Data: Basic technical information (e.g., device model, OS version) may be used for troubleshooting.

2. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

All financial transactions are processed securely by Apple Inc. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing address.

The purchase data we receive is used exclusively to:

  • Verify your "Premium" status and unlock content;
  • Facilitate the "Restore Purchases" function across your Apple devices;
  • Comply with Apple’s accounting and tax requirements.

3. Data Usage and Sharing

Since we prioritize your privacy:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties or advertisers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or identifiers to track your behavior across other apps or websites.
  • Service Providers: We use Apple as our sole service provider for app distribution and payment processing.

4. Data Storage

Your app preferences (e.g., favorite sounds, timer settings) are stored locally on your device. Subscription data is managed by Apple and synced via your iCloud account to ensure you can access your purchase on any authorized device.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All purchase activities should be conducted with parental consent or through Apple’s "Ask to Buy" feature.

6. Your Rights (Standard EULA)

By using this App, you also agree to the Apple Standard End User License Agreement (EULA). You can manage or cancel your subscriptions at any time through your iOS System Settings.
