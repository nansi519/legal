Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Welcome to Mood: Visual Soundscapes (“the App”). We value your privacy. This App is designed to provide a serene experience with minimal data footprint. We do not require account registration, and you do not need to provide your name, email address, phone number, or precise location to use the App. We do not operate our own servers for this App. Most App features work without an internet connection. Internet access may be required only for Apple services such as in-app purchases, subscription verification, and restoring purchases.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, to provide premium features through In-App Purchases, we collect certain non-identifiable data:

  • Purchase History: We record your subscription status, transaction IDs, and purchase dates provided by Apple.
  • Device Data: We do not operate our own servers and do not collect device information ourselves. Basic device information may be available to Apple through App Store Connect diagnostics and crash reports if you choose to share analytics with Apple.

2. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

All financial transactions are processed securely by Apple Inc. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing address.

The purchase data we receive is used exclusively to:

  • Verify your "Premium" status and unlock content;
  • Facilitate the "Restore Purchases" function across your Apple devices;

3. Data Usage and Sharing

Since we prioritize your privacy:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not use third-party analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties or advertisers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or identifiers to track your behavior across other apps or websites.
  • Service Providers: We use Apple as our sole service provider for app distribution and payment processing.

4. Data Storage

Your app preferences (favorites, alarm settings, timer preferences, playback preferences, and usage insights) are stored locally on your device. You can delete locally stored data by deleting the App from your device. Subscription records are managed by Apple and can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All purchase activities should be conducted with parental consent or through Apple’s "Ask to Buy" feature.

Please also review our Terms of Service, which govern your use of the App.
